Runi

Runi is a Mobile App developed during the course Multimedia Laboratory 5 by me and my friends Paula Pires and Reinaldo Simões.

This app was designed for people who some times have a hard time deciding what they are going to cook. Runi is a platform where people can share their recipes with others and try something different sometimes, and will doing it, raise their cooking game. We also give the option to search by the name of recipe or the category!

This project was developed using Cordova framework(frontend) and PHP(backend).

Unfortunately the demo of this app is closed inside of infrastructer of my university !
